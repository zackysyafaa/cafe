About Us

More than
just coffee.

Moment Social Space was born from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee, a beautiful space, and good company can change your entire day.

We source our beans directly from local farmers across Sumatra, and our kitchen celebrates the bold flavors of Nusantara. Whether you're here to hustle, unwind, or meet someone new — there is always a corner for you.
